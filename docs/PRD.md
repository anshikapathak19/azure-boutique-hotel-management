# AzureStay - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Product Overview

AzureStay is a premium cloud-native Boutique Hotel Booking and Management Platform designed to provide a seamless hospitality experience through modern web technologies and Microsoft Azure cloud services.

The platform enables guests to discover and reserve luxury accommodations while allowing hotel staff and administrators to efficiently manage hotel operations through dedicated management portals.

---

# 2. Vision

To redefine boutique hospitality by combining luxury guest experiences with intelligent cloud-powered hotel management.

---

# 3. Mission

Deliver a premium hotel management platform that simplifies reservations, room management, guest services, and hotel operations using Microsoft Azure.

---

# 4. Target Users

## Guest

People looking to book luxury rooms and manage their stay.

### Capabilities

- Register
- Login
- Browse Rooms
- Book Rooms
- Cancel Bookings
- View Booking History
- Save Preferences
- Contact Hotel

---

## Hotel Staff

Hotel employees responsible for day-to-day operations.

### Capabilities

- Manage Check-ins
- Manage Check-outs
- Update Room Status
- Housekeeping Management
- Guest Requests
- View Daily Reservations

---

## Administrator

Hotel Manager with complete system control.

### Capabilities

- Manage Rooms
- Manage Bookings
- Manage Customers
- Manage Staff
- Analytics Dashboard
- Hotel Configuration
- Revenue Insights
